About Me


Giving birth has a deep psychological impact. It touches us on a level that few experiences can. Planning for a birth that is not only safe and healthy, but one that is consonant with your value system and fits your family’s way of life creates a foundation for the long-term physical and emotional wellbeing of parents and children. During my six pregnancies, it was vitally important to me to be able to engage with my providers in planning birth experiences that helped me feel safe, supported, and involved in the process. As a coach, it is my goal to empower you to do the same. My professional training and experience in psychology and wellness coaching, in combination with my Birth Coach Method and Lamaze certifications, have prepared me to help expectant parents explore their beliefs and fears, clarify their values and preferences, and identify ways to achieve their goals.
