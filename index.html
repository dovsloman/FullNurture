What Is Transformational Birth Coaching?


We live in a world where there is instant access to endless information coming from a wide variety of sources. This can be a wonderful resource, but it can also lead to overload. It can be difficult to know which of the many competing voices to listen to, when even acknowledged authorities disagree. Adding to the confusion is a battleground of conflicting opinions on the subject in which each represents their favorite approach as the one “right” way to give birth.

In truth, a safe and healthy birth can be achieved in many different ways, and most decisions about the process are really a matter of personal values, beliefs, preferences, and practicalities.

The purpose of transformational birth coaching is to empower you to clarify and articulate your birth vision, explore your values and beliefs about birth, identify your options, and put into practice the actions necessary to enable your goals.
